We@t - Make better food choice

We@t is a web-app that recommends restaurants to the users based on the current weather condition. This web-app only works in three cities: Oakland, San Francisco, and Berkeley. 
Start Searching
After typing the location on the search bar, the website will show you a list of restaurants based on the weather. 
You can register your name, email, and location to receive an email about the recommendation restaurants every day at 8 am.
